Crisis Text Line

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

Take a few minutes each day to focus on your breath and be present in the moment. This can help reduce stress and anxiety.

Physical Activity

Regular exercise can reduce symptoms of depression and anxiety while improving mood and overall well-being.
